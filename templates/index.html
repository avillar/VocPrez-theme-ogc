You are looking at the system home page of an instance of VocPrez. It is a system designed to deliver SKOS vocabularies (sometimes called taxonomies) on the Internet in both human- and machine-readable forms.

See the About page for more information.

Starting Points

This instance of VocPrez is configured specifically to deliver the OGC's vocabularies. This means the particular options available and the models of information may be slightly different from other VocPrez instances.

The OGC conceives of SKOS vocabularies as being distinct concept schemes so that the list of 'vocabularies' is a list of skos:ConceptScheme instances. See the full list:
