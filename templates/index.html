Welcome to the OGC Definitions Server.

OGC publishes definitions using the SKOS vocabulary (or "taxonomy") model in both human- and machine-readable forms.

Some definitions have richer information models with alternative views available.

Vocabularies are managed in sets called "ConceptSchemes", and definitions can usually be accessed individually as "Concepts" or as a set.

The "alternates view" will provide a complete list of the available resources per definition.
